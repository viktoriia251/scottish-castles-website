Castle Gallery

Explore the beauty and history of Scotland's most iconic castles. Below are some of the most famous castles, each with a unique story and architectural style.

Edinburgh Castle

One of Scotland's most iconic landmarks, standing tall on Castle Rock with centuries of history.

Stirling Castle

A majestic fortress that played a key role in Scotland's battles for independence.

Urquhart Castle

Overlooking Loch Ness, this medieval ruin is steeped in legend and mystery.

Dundonald Castle

Once home to Scottish kings, this hilltop fortress offers breathtaking countryside views.

Eilean Donan Castle

Scotland's most photographed castle, standing proudly where three lochs meet.

Culzean Castle

A stunning clifftop castle with grand architecture and sweeping coastal views.
